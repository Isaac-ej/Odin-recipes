Fish Batter

Unbelievable Fish Batter

This fish batter recipe makes the lightest, crispiest batter imaginable! I received this recipe on a talk radio show in the early 1950s. It makes enough to coat approximately 2 pounds of fish.

Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour ½ cup milk ½ cup water ¼ teaspoon baking powder 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

Step 1 Whisk flour, milk, water, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl until smooth. To Use Heat oil in a deep fryer to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C). Dip fish fillets into the batter to coat. Carefully lower fillets, one at a time, into the hot oil. Fry several fillets at a time, turning once, until cooked through and golden brown, about 2 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
